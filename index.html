ABOUT US

Baseri Barber Shop in Twickenham, London specialises in the art of traditional gentlemen's barber services. We are easily accessible via public transport with a main high street bus stop and 2 mins walk from Twickenham station, you may also cycle and drive with parking available 1 min walk away.

EXTENSIVE SKILL-SET
The barbers are particularly skillful in 'Skin Fade' ensuring a sharp transition , 'Beard Trim' and 'Re-styling'.

ITS A PASSION
With a passion for the trade, Baseri barbers are able to recommend new trending hairstyles, expert advice on maintaining healthy hair and your beard.

OUR GOALS FOR YOU
Baseri Barber Shop aims to provide quality service that is balanced between good morals, value and long lasting relationships.
